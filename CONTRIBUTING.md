# Contributing to Antrain

Thank you for your interest in Antrain!

## Portfolio Project

**Antrain is currently a personal portfolio and showcase project.** It was developed as a demonstration of:
- Modern iOS development with Swift 6 and SwiftUI
- Clean Architecture principles
- Protocol-Oriented Design
- Privacy-first app development
